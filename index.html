WHAT IS TED?

TED is a non-profit dedicated to ideas worth spreading. TED stands for Technology, Entertainment, and Design – three areas that are collectively shaping our future. Primarily, TED organises events where the world’s leading thinkers and doers are invited to share their ideas. These talks are recorded and uploaded to reach a larger audience. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Sal Khan, Daniel Kahneman, and many other inspiring leaders.

WHAT IS TEDx?

In 2007, TED started a program called TEDx (x = independently organised event). TEDx events are licensed programs that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. With the advent of TEDx events, audiences around the world have been gathering for deep discussions about innovative ideas. TEDx is designed to give communities and individuals the opportunity to stimulate dialogue through TED-like experiences at a local level. At these events, people from the community take the stage to inspire others and thus are shaping the future of the community.


WHAT IS TEDx WLU Brantford?

TEDxWLUBrantford is a student run event focusing on delivering inspiring ideas to a global platform while also improving our community. Created in the spirit of TED’s mission, “Ideas worth spreading,” the event is designed to give the Brantford community, business organizations and students the opportunity to stimulate dialogue through locally organized TED-like experiences. Live speakers and TED Talk videos are curated by a highly qualified selected team to challenge perspectives and spark deep discussion among attendees, speakers and students. The goal of this event is to inspire all participants to realize and unlock their infinite potential to create change.
